# Game Overview
You are a retired wizard running a magical restaurant powered by your fire-breathing dragon. Your goal is to survive each shift by cooking meals, serving customers, and keeping your dragon happy enough to keep the grill running.

# Objective
Complete each shift by serving the required number of customers without:
- Losing 3 customers  
- Letting your dragon become too unhappy (shutdown/tantrum)

# Gameplay Walkthrough
1. Start the game from the title screen  
2. In the tutorial level, use WASD to move around the restaurant  
3. Walk to the cooler and press E to pick up raw food  
4. Walk to the stove and press E to open the cooking minigame  
5. Drag raw food onto the grill and wait for it to cook  
6. Pick up a plate from the shelf  
7. Collect the cooked food from the stove  
8. If the order is a burger:
   - Wait for a burger ticket  
   - Use the topping station to add toppings  
9. Bring the completed meal to the counter and press E to serve it  
10. Between orders, interact with the dragon to manage hunger, boredom, and tiredness  
11. Press Space to interrupt the goblin if it tries to steal food  
12. Complete enough orders to finish the level  

Avoid burning food three times or letting the dragon become too unhappy.

# Core Gameplay Loop
- View customer orders  
- Cook food using the dragon-powered stove  
- Plate and serve meals efficiently  
- Manage your dragon’s needs between orders  
- Prevent goblin from stealing food  
- Survive the shift  

# Dragon Management
Your dragon powers the grill, but only if it’s happy.

The dragon has four key needs:
- Hunger – feed it  
- Boredom – interact/pet it  
- Tiredness – give it a blanket  
- Happiness – overall indicator of its state  

If the dragon becomes too unhappy, the grill shuts down and you can’t cook anymore.

# Cooking Mechanics

# Grill Station
- Place meat on the dragon-powered grill  
- Watch the cooking timer carefully  
- Avoid overcooking or undercooking  

3 mistakes results in a loss.

# Plating Station
- Add required toppings/sides  
- Match the customer’s order  
- Serve the finished plate  

# Controls
Move – WASD  
Interact – E  
Drag & Drop – Mouse  
Pause Menu – Esc  
Interrupt Goblin – Space  

# Cheat Codes
Shift – Skip level  
Alt + 1 – Unlock level 1  
Alt + 2 – Unlock level 2  

# End State
The game ends if:
- The player burns food 3 times  
- The player fails to maintain the dragon  
- The player successfully completes the levels
