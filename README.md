# EnterTheDrag-Inn
Collaborative game project for ITCS4230/ITCS5230

Game Name: Enter the Dragon-Inn
Team Name: Dragons
Names: Amman Kamili, Ethan-Bradley-Foo, Nathan Pereira, Hayes Bridges


Overview
You are a retired wizard running a magical restaurant powered by your fire-breathing dragon. Your goal is to survive each shift by cooking meals, serving customers, and keeping your dragon happy enough to keep the grill running.

Objective
Complete a shift by serving the required number of customers without:
Losing 3 customers
Letting your dragon become too unhappy (shut down / tantrum)
Walkthrough
1. Start the game from the title screen.
2. In the tutorial level, use WASD to move around the restaurant.
3. Walk to the cooler and press E to pick up raw food.
4. Walk to the stove and press E to open the cooking minigame.
5. Drag the raw food onto the grill and wait for it to cook.
6. Pick up a plate from the shelf, then collect the cooked food from the stove.
7. If the order is a burger, wait for a burger ticket, then use the topping station to add toppings.
8. Bring the completed meal to the counter and press E to serve it.
9. Between orders, interact with the dragon to manage hunger, boredom, and tiredness.
10. Press Space to interrupt the goblin if it tries to steal food.
11. Complete enough orders to finish the level. Avoid burning food three times or letting the dragon become too unhappy.

Core Gameplay Loop
View customer orders
Cook food using the dragon-powered stove
Plate and serve meals efficiently
Manage your dragon’s needs between orders
Prevent goblin from stealing food
Survive the shift
Dragon Management
Your dragon powers the grill, but only if it’s happy.
The dragon has 4 key needs:
Hunger – feed it
Boredom – interact/pet it
Tiredness – give it a blanket
Happiness – overall indicator of its state
If the dragon becomes too unhappy, the grill shuts down and you can’t cook anymore. 

Cooking is done through simple minigames:
Grill Station
Place meat on the dragon-powered grill
Watch the cooking timer carefully
Avoid overcooking or undercooking or you will gain a strike. 3 strikes and you lose!
Plating Station
Add required toppings/sides
Match the customer’s order
Serve the finished plate
Controls
WASD – Move around the restaurant
E – Interact with stations and customers
Mouse – Drag & drop in cooking minigames
Esc - Pause Menu
Space - Interrupt Goblin
Cheatcode
Shift - Skip level
alt + 1 or alt + 2 unlocks level 1 and 2 respectively 
End State
If the player burns the food 3 times, fails to maintain the dragon, or successfully manages to complete the levels, then the game will end and display the game over screen.
